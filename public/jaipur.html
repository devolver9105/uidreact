RUBY University , JAIPUR

The beginnings of RUBY UNIVERSITY can be traced to 1994 when a School of Engineering was started on the lands where Royal Kings gave their lives to protect our Mother India from the Intruders in Jaipur, at the foothills of Aravalli ranges in the Jaipur district of Rajasthan.At that time there were 120 students and 13 faculty members. Today, RUBY has three campuses in three different states of India. There is a student population of over 12,000 and faculty strength of nearly 1500. Over 120 UG, PG and doctoral programs are offered. When RUBY became a “deemed to be University” in January 2003, it was the youngest group of institutions to be conferred this status. As Ruby's first campus, jaipur is home to the administrative headquarters of RUBY university.

Schools of Engineering and Business, in addition to Departments of Social Work and Communication, are located on this campus. Research centers include AMBE, CEN, CES and Cybersecurity. The campus has nearly 3500 students and about 600 faculty and staff members. This sprawling 400-acre campus is often likened to an oasis in a desert. Massive tree-planting and forestation activities undertaken over the past decade have converted the once-barren land into a lush green and beautiful place. A lot of emphasis is placed on eco-friendly and conservation practices. Waste water from the hostels and faculty and staff quarters is treated using EM; no chemicals are used in the process. Organic fertilizers are made using vermi-compost, pesticides have been avoided since the very beginning. The tranquil surroundings, away from the pollution and the bustle of city life, are home to several exotic birds, including parrots and peacocks.

Infrastructure

The 400 acre campus is well planned with infrastructure like academic buildings, adminstration wing, central library, hostels, guest house, staff-quarters, shopping complex, swimming pool and play grounds. The campus has built-up area of over 28 lakhs sq. feet linked by 7 km black topped roads. Eight hostels accomodated 5000 students. A swimming pool meeting Olympic standards is utlized by students, staff and others in the campus. Two power houses are installed with total generating capacity of 1660KVA to meet the shortage in power supply. Ruby also provides uninterrupted power and water supply round the clock.

Xtreme- WorldWide Multifest

Welcome to XTREME, the annual extravaganza is a three-day national level inter-collegiate multi-fest hosted by Ruby University, where the campus transforms into a haven of excitement, creativity, and boundless energy. XTREME isn't just a college fest; it's a celebration of passion, talent, and the indomitable spirit of youth. As students flood into the Ruby University campus, they are greeted by a kaleidoscope of colors, pulsating music, and an atmosphere charged with anticipation. The theme of XTREME permeates every aspect of the fest, from the adrenaline-pumping events to the captivating decorations that adorn the venue. Towering rock-climbing walls, BMX stunt ramps, and zip lines crisscrossing the sky create an electrifying ambiance, promising thrills and excitement at every turn. From extreme sports competitions like parkour and skateboarding to daring stunts and adrenaline-fueled challenges, participants push themselves beyond their limits, cheered on by fellow students and spectators alike. The roar of the crowd mingles with the sound of engines revving and boards hitting the pavement, creating an exhilarating atmosphere that epitomizes the spirit of XTREME.

But XTREME isn't just about physical prowess; it's also a showcase of creativity, innovation, and artistic expression. Tech enthusiasts demonstrate their ingenuity in drone racing competitions and robotics challenges, while aspiring filmmakers screen their adrenaline-fueled short films in outdoor cinemas under the stars. Art installations and graffiti walls provide a canvas for students to unleash their imagination, turning the RUBY University campus into a vibrant tapestry of colors and ideas.

One of the highlights of XTREME is its lineup of guest speakers and performers who embody the spirit of adventure and exploration. Renowned athletes such as Alex Honnold, known for his daring free solo climbs, and Bethany Hamilton, the inspirational surfer who overcame adversity to return to the waves after a shark attack, share their experiences and insights, inspiring the next generation of champions to pursue their dreams fearlessly. Musical acts from various genres keep the energy levels soaring late into the night, with electrifying performances by artists like DJ Marshmello and rock bands like Imagine Dragons that leave the audience craving for more. On the Indian front, XTREME showcases the best of the country's talent. Musical acts from various genres keep the energy levels soaring late into the night, with electrifying performances by artists like Sunidhi Chauhan, the powerhouse vocalist who captivates audiences with her soulful renditions, and Zakir Hussain, the tabla maestro whose rhythms transport listeners to another realm. These Indian artists bring a unique flavor to the fest, infusing it with the vibrant rhythms and melodies of their culture. Renowned athletes such as Virat Kohli and MS Dhoni, the cricketing legends known for their resilience and leadership on the field, share their experiences and insights, inspiring the next generation of champions to pursue their dreams fearlessly.

None of this would be possible without the dedication and hard work of the organizing committee and volunteers who pour their heart and soul into bringing XTREME to life. Months of planning, countless meetings, and sleepless nights culminate in a spectacle that captivates and inspires all who attend. As the sun sets on another exhilarating day at XTREME, and exhausted but elated participants make their way home, what remains is not just memories of epic feats and heart-pounding moments, but a sense of camaraderie, achievement, and the unshakable belief that with courage and determination, anything is possible at RUBY University's XTREME college fest.
